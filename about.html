About 103 Creations

Crafted with Soul, Build for Meaning.

At 103 Creations, we believe every product should tell a story, especially your story. We are a Malaysian-born brand under HA Innovative Store (SSM 202403084009), creating perfume, gifting, engraving and books that connect people through meaning, beauty and personal touch.

Our name carries a simple promise: Every creations must feel special. Whether it's the scent of your favourite moments, a personalised gift that makes someone smile or a book that changes the way you see the world. We craft it with care, precision and purpose.

Our DNA

We are part of a bigger dream. The 103 Arcology Project. A multi-brand ecosystem aimed at empowering communities, creating opportunities and building a sustainable future for Malaysia.

At our core, we live by the values of Santun (respect & gentleness), Amanah (trustworthiness) and Quiet Excellence by delivering work that speaks for itself.

When you support 103 Creations, you’re not just buying a product. You’re joining a movement to bring more meaning, beauty and kindness into the world.
