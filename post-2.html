The Story of REVV103

Some brands are born from profit. Ours was born from purpose.

The number 103 isn’t random. It’s a marker of a very precious date, a silent witness to moments that changed everything for our founder, a symbol of new beginnings, quiet resilience and a promise to build something lasting.

What started as a small idea in a living room by crafting gifts for loved ones (hobby) then grew into WeGoRandom and now officially known as 103 Creations, a brand that believes every item should mean something. We don’t just sell products. We engrave stories, bottle emotions and design moments you can hold in your hand.

Our creations live in three worlds:

    1. Perfumes that carry your personality in every note.

    2. Personalised gifts that speak without words.

    3. Books that hold wisdom, imagination, and soul.

Every product passes through the same hands that dreamed of making this brand a reality by ensuring it’s personal, premium and purposeful.

But 103 Creations is also part of something bigger. The 103 Arcology Project a vision to build not just a business, but a community and legacy that uplifts others. From small gifts to global ideas, everything we make is a step towards that dream.

Because here, you’re not just buying a product. You’re carrying a story and now, it’s yours to tell.

Who it’s for?

Riders, builders, people who prefer progress over noise. REVV103 is subtle up close, steady over hours, and never loud in a room.
